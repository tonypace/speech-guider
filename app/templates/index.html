How to use Comparison Lab

  1. Type your target text and wait for the reference to generate
  2. Click "Record" and speak the text
  3. Scrub or play to compare articulatory movements side-by-side
  4. Use "Link Playheads" to synchronize timelines
  5. Adjust speed and zoom to inspect details
  6. Set loop region to repeat difficult sections

Instructions

  1. Read the target sentence aloud
  2. Click "Choose File" to upload a .wav file (or use the microphone)
  3. Click "Analyze Pronunciation" to receive feedback
  4. Select an error to see articulatory visualization
  5. Click "Animate" to see mouth/tongue positions
  6. Switch to "Animation Lab" tab to experiment with vocal tract shapes
  7. Switch to "Prosody Lab" to compare rhythm and intonation across recordings
  8. Switch to "Comparison Lab" to compare your articulation against reference

Note: This system uses IPA-based phoneme analysis. For best results, speak clearly and at a moderate pace.
